The SignUp Google Chrome
Extension overlays ASL
interpretation on the streaming
platform, Disney+, for more
accessible captioning. SignUp was
founded because many young
people from the Deaf community
find written captions indescriptive,
or even absent from media sites. A
2017 study showed that 80% of
children who signed face challenges
with reading - making
written captions inaccessible. It will
also be used as a learning tool for ASL
students. SignUp is a fun way for kids
(and adults!) to enjoy mainstream
movies.

FQAs

How do I install SignUp?

Download SignUp Chrome Extension

Click on one of the download buttons above and they will
take you to the download page. Alternatively, you can
access the extension on the Google Chrome Extension
store by searching ‘SignUp.’ Either way, there is a one-click
installation process.

How does SignUp work?

  1. Open disneyplus.com
  2. Select which movie to watch and press ‘play’
  3. Open the Google Chrome extension from the Chrome
    Browser
  4. A pop-up will appear with simple directions
  5. A bar on the right will appear with a list of
    alphabetized movies; click ‘add’ on the movie of your
    choice and confirm
  6. A pop-up box containing the ASL video will appear,
    and the user can change the size and location of the
    box
  7. If you wish to exit the extension, an ‘exit’ button will
    appear if the extension is clicked on the Chrome
    Browser or if you click the ‘x’ on the corner of the
    video box
  8. Grab the popcorn and enjoy the movie!
